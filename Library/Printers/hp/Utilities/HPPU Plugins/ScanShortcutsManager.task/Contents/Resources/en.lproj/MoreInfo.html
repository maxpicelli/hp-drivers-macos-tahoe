Scan to Computer lets you start scans at your HP device and automatically send the output to your computer.

To use Scan to Computer, make sure that Enable Scan to Computer is selected.

With HP Easy Scan (or HP Scan for some products) installed, you also have the ability to view and edit scanning tasks. To view and edit the scanning tasks, click the Edit Scan Tasks button. To download and install the latest software for your device, visit http://www.hp.com/support.

If your HP device is shared with others, you may be required to select your computer name on the device when you start a scan. This is the Computer Name that is specified in your Mac's System Preferences. If you don't remember the name, or if you would like to ensure that it is unique and easily recognizable, click Change Computer Name.
