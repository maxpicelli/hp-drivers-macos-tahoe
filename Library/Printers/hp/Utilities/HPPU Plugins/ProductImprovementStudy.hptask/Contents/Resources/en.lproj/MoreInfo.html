More Information

Participation requires Internet access and your approval to collect and transmit the usage data to HP. The information that will be collected and transmitted to HP is listed below.

Periodic sharing will occur automatically when connected.

Information Collected:

  • Basic information about your Mac: Operating system, locale, time zone
  • Basic printer identification: Model number, serial number
  • Print volumes: Number/type of cartridges, types of pages printed
  • Printing events: Cartridge Low warnings
  • What features of your printer you are using: Photo cards, fax, scan, Embedded Web Server
  • Software used to print: Application names and number of pages printed.
