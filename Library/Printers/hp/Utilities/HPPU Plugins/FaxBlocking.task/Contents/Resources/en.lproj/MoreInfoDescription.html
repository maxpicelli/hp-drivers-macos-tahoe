The Junk Fax Blocking settings pane is used to enter a list of fax numbers you want to block from sending faxes to your device.

Junk Fax Blocking requires that your device is connected to a line with Caller ID enabled.

If you are unsure if your line has Caller ID enabled, please contact your telephone service provider.

Click “Print List” to print a report of the numbers currently being blocked.

HINT: You can shift-click and command-click to make multiple selections.
