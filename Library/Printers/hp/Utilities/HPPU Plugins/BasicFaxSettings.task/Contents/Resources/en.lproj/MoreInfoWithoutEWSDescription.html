The Basic Fax Settings pane is used to enter the settings your HP device needs to send and receive faxes.

The "Company/Name" and "Fax Number" fields will be printed on the header at the top of each page you send via fax. A station name and sender fax number are required for your device to send faxes. To continue, please complete the "Company/Name" and "Fax Number" fields.###MoreInfoFaxDialPrefix###

Rings Until Answer allows the device to automatically answer incoming calls after the specific number of rings.

To test the fax line connected to your device, click the "Test the Device Fax Setup" button.

NOTE: Refer to the device documentation for more information.
